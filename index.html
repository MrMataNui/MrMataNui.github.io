Martian

Started on 2017.08.23

A while ago, I started creating a conlang. The grammar and most of the words were generated by Vulgarlang.

Here is an example sentence: DOZAC LWA LWZWQW OROIAJ XWHEXW

IPA: /ʃɔʒæɲə ləæ ləˈʒəxə ɔɾˈɔiæʤ ʧəˈʔeʧə/

Romanization: Shozhanyë lëa lëzhëkhë oroiaj chë'echë

Translation: I'm making a language called Martian
